Sky

Wednesday, April 30, 2014

 

People claim they see Heaven on Earth

In some of the most beautiful and extravagant places

In reality heaven is right before our eyes

Some days heaven is a mystery,

A shroud of wispy clouds are strewn across endless blue

The sky protects our naivety and shields us from dangers,

Like the warm protective wings of a mother bird

but it also brings about a strong curiosity in mankind

Everyone wants to see what utopia lays beyond the sheath of blue

Or brilliant blanket of stars

But somedays I’m content right where I am,

Gazing across the horizon I’m in my own heaven on earth

Why ask for more?
